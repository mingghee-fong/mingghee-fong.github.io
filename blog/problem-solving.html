Problem Solving Technical Blog

1. Time when I was blocked on a simple problem:

What was the problem?
-Choosing a method to solve a question. It's like you are going out for dinner, but you can't decide what attire to wear. Essentially they all served the purpose of not going out naked but the thing is, which one is more suitable.

What problem-solving techniques did I use?
-I went with having multiple choices, wrote the code out then commented out all of them except for one and repeat the process. Then I will pick the one that I will understand the most. Much like, you have all your outfit ready but you are thinking what sort of occasion it is that you are attending and whichever you feel more appeal to your mood that day, you pick that one.

How did I feel throughout the process?
-Exhausted and beaten. I was literally staring at the screen and at the same questions for hours.

What did I learn?
-Don't waste time, don't be stubborn. Ask for help if I am really struggling.
